Game Rules:

The first player to reach the total score of at least 50 wins! Each player has to roll the dice on his / her turn, the 'current' score will add up until the player hits the button 'hold' (then the current score is added to the total score of the player) or until the dice shows the number 1 (then the current score gets set to zero and the turn is changed to the other player).
